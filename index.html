Can I use Clickt even if my team is small?

Yes. Clickt works well for solo builders, small teams, and growing organizations because you can start with one workflow and scale at your own pace.

  • Start with a single project in Teams + Checklist.
  • Add Builder and Presentation only when reporting needs grow.
  • Keep one system even as more teammates join.
View 60-sec workflow See small-team examples
Do I need to replace all my tools on day one?

No. Most teams adopt Clickt incrementally and keep their current stack while migrating one repeatable workflow at a time.

  • Week 1: move planning and ownership into Teams.
  • Week 2: standardize recurring steps in Checklist.
  • Later: connect data analysis and presentations for reporting cycles.

Where does ClicktAI fit in daily work?

ClicktAI helps at both kickoff and reporting time by turning natural-language prompts into actionable workflow steps across modules.

  • Kickoff: create task/checklist structure from a goal prompt.
  • Mid-cycle: speed up analysis and insight drafting from imported data.
  • Closeout: help prepare presentation-ready updates faster.
